# Auditor Link – Quick User Guide

**Link:** https://www.samavedacapital.in/auditor

---

## Step 1: Enter Your Details

1. Open the link above in your browser.
2. Enter **your name** (e.g. John Doe).
3. Enter **your company name** (e.g. Acme Corp).
4. Click **Continue**.

---

## Step 2: Upload Your Documents

1. Click **Upload Files** to add one or more files.
2. Or click **Upload Folders** to add a whole folder with its structure.
3. (Optional) Click **New Folder** to create folders and organize files.
4. Wait until all uploads finish. You’ll see a list of your files.

---

## Step 3: Choose What You Need

You’ll see three tabs. Use the one you need:

### Forensic Audit
- Click **Start Audit**.
- Wait a few minutes. The AI will analyze your documents.
- When done, the report appears on screen.
- Click **Download PDF** to save it.

### CIM (Confidential Information Memorandum)
- Click **Generate CIM**.
- Wait for it to finish.
- Preview the CIM on screen.
- Click **Download PDF** to save it.

### Teaser
- Click **Generate Teaser**.
- Wait for it to finish.
- Preview the teaser on screen.
- Click **Download PDF** to save it.

---

## Tips

- **Supported formats:** PDF, Word, Excel, images.
- **Large files:** Upload may take a while. Don’t close the page.
- **Errors:** If something fails, try again. If it keeps failing, contact support.
- **Same session:** You can return to the same link later. Your session is saved in the browser.
